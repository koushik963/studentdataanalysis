Student Data Analysis

This project shows the analysis of the University of Akron undergraduate students data from the past 10 years. Depending on the admit type of the students the application is classified into the following types:

  • Freshman Data
  • All other admit types

Student Data Analysis-Freshman Data

The Freshman data are classified as following

  • Ethnicity-related Analysis
  • Students having ACT Scores Above Average categorized by Ethnicity
  • Students having GPA at Completion above 3.5 categorized by Ethnicity
  • ACT Scores-related Analysis
  • Students having ACT Scores Above Average categorized by Ethnicity
  • Students having ACT Scores above Average categorized by Departments
  • GPA-related Analysis
  • Students having GPA at Completion above average categorised by Departments
  • Students having GPA at Completion above average categorised by Ethnicity
  • High School-related Analysis
  • Students having ACT Scores above average categorized by High School
  • Students Distribution categorised by high school
  • Students Distribution categorised by County
